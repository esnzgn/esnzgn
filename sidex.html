About Me

I am a computational biologist and digital artist focused on cancer systems biology, multi-omics data integration, and statistical modeling. I currently work as a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Helsinki.

My research is centered around identifying cancer vulnerabilities and synthetic lethality interactions using data-driven and network-based approaches.

Featured Projects

  • CoPISA – Co-perturbation analysis framework for cancer drug targets using thermal profiling.
  • NIMAA – R package for bipartite network imputation and modularity analysis with missing data.
  • PepBind – Peptide-compound interaction predictor using graph-sequence neural networks.
  • StatVisuals – A Shiny-based educational tool for statistical and probabilistic concepts.
